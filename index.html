Harmony Hub

Discover Your Next Favorite Song with Personalized Music Recommendations

Key Features

Personalized Song Recommendations

Based on the songs you've liked, our algorithm curates a playlist of new music tailored to your unique taste. Explore new artists and tracks that resonate with your preferences.

Interactive Music Dashboard

Dive into a visually engaging interface that displays your music preferences and listening habits. Track your favorite genres, artists, and see how your taste evolves over time.

Social Sharing and Playlists

Share your custom playlists with friends on social media and discover what they're listening to. Collaborate on playlists and find common musical ground with your peers.

About

Growing up, music was always a significant part of my life. My father used to play his vinyl collection on Sunday mornings, filling our home with the sounds of classic rock and jazz. As I got older, I found myself exploring different genres and discovering new artists, often feeling overwhelmed by the sheer volume of music available.

I wanted to create a tool that would make it easier for people to find new music they love, just as I found my way to new favorites through my father's records. This project is a tribute to those cherished Sunday mornings and a way to help others navigate the vast world of music.
